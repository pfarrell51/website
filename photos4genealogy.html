Andrew Farrell Obit, Cedar Rapids Evening Gazette 4-25-1892
Andrew Farrell who was seriously injured in the C.M.& St. P.(*) yards at Marion while coupling cars and was taken to St. Lukes hospital where is foot was amputated, died at 8:15 last night. He was injured April 14th and was taken to the hospital about a week ago. His condition became so critical that amputation was absolutely necessary and then it was only a chance that his life could be saved. His foot was taken off last Thursday just above the ankle, but the loss of blood and complications which had already begun, resulted in his death. He will be taken this evening to his home in Dunlap Iowa

* (C.M.&St P was the Chicago, Milwaukee and St Paul railroad)

Daniel J Farrell (b. 1860) Obit

Thursday November 1, 1934 Omaha World-Herald Omaha Nebraska Page 22

In South Omaha Daniel J Farrell, 74, retired building contractor, died Wedensday night of heart trouble at his home, 2517 G Street, after a brief illness. He had lived in Omaha since 1887.

Surviving neside the widow are six children: Daniel, jr., William P., Mary and Andrew F Harrell, and Mrs, H. L. Dahlquist, all of Omaha, and Mrs John N Ruther of Nebraska City. The funeral will be held from the residence at 8:30 a.m., Friday to St Bridgets's church at 9 o'clock, and burial will be in St. Mary's cemetery.
