Trails/Enduro

Post August 23, 2016.
Trails

Trail/enduro. My first motorcycle was a Hodaka Ace 90, I bought it with the earnings of my work in the summer of 1969. It was a "dirt bike" altho it nominally was street legal.

I got out of motorcycles in about 1985, did not ride one again for about 30 years. Then I slowly got back into it. Last Summer, I bought a new enduro bike, a KTM 300 XC-W.
